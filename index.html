Online Storefront

Each seller gets a fully separate store — own domain, product catalogue, customer accounts, and order history. No data crosses between tenants.

Walk-in Billing (POS)

Counter billing with product search, discount codes, combo pricing, GST calculation, and printable PDF receipts. Designed to run on basic hardware.

Inventory Management

Stock by size, colour, and weight. Automatic low-stock alerts. Restock tracking. Every seller sees only their own inventory.

Payments & Shipping
